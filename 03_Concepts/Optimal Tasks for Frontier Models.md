# **Optimal Tasks for Frontier Models**

Language models demonstrate high reliability when tasked with specific, bounded operations during preprocessing. They excel at topic segmentation, capable of reading a massive wall of transcript text and injecting accurate Markdown headers, thereby transforming unreadable blocks into scannable, chunk-ready sections.30 They are highly proficient at metadata generation, accurately reading a document to output formatted YAML frontmatter that extracts the author, date, core entities, and suggested taxonomy tags.30 Furthermore, instructing a model to extract all verifiable claims and the exact verbatim quotes that support them yields highly reliable reference notes that preserve the integrity of the original source.

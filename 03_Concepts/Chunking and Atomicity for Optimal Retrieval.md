# **Chunking and Atomicity for Optimal Retrieval**

In Retrieval-Augmented Generation applications, the language model does not read the entire vault simultaneously; it reads discrete chunks of text retrieved by a vector database based on semantic similarity to the user's query. Therefore, the physical boundaries of the text dictate the quality of the model's reasoning. Poor chunking destroys contextual coherence.23

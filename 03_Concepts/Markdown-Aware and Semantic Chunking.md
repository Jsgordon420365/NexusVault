# **Markdown-Aware and Semantic Chunking**

Because Obsidian data is inherently structured in Markdown, the optimal chunking strategy leverages document structure. Markdown-aware chunking instructs the parser to split the document exclusively at heading boundaries. This ensures that a specific topic remains contextually bound together.23 More advanced pipelines utilize semantic chunking, which groups sentences based on embedding similarity, identifying natural shifts in topic or theme before mathematically establishing a chunk boundary.23

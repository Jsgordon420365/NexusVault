# **Limitations in High-Volume Contexts**

While highly adaptable, Obsidian is not a native relational database and presents specific limitations when scaled indiscriminately. It is not optimized for real-time, concurrent multi-user editing, nor is it inherently built to handle millions of rows of structured tabular data. In artificial intelligence workflows, a vault containing tens of thousands of disorganized, poorly chunked notes will overwhelm a language model's context window and severely degrade retrieval accuracy.8 Obsidian excels as a qualitative knowledge graph, but requires rigorous structural discipline and external preprocessing to function as a reliable, machine-readable substrate at scale.

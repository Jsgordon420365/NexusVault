# **The Dangers of Arbitrary Splitting**

Standard recursive character text splitting—the default in many basic AI frameworks—arbitrarily slices text at fixed token intervals. This primitive method frequently cuts paragraphs in half, severs a pronoun from its preceding noun, or separates a factual claim from its vital qualifying caveat. This forces the language model to reason over incomplete fragments, inevitably resulting in hallucinated context to fill the gaps.23
